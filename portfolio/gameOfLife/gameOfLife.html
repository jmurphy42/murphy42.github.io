Instructions ▽

  • Click the grid to create a beginning world.
  • Click 'RUN' to start the game of life.
  • Cells will become alive, persist, and die, as determined by this set of rules:
    • Living cells touching 0-1 cells are lonely. They die.
    • Living cells touching 2-3 cells are happy. They persist.
    • Living cells touching 4+ cells are crowded. They die.
    • Other cells touching 3 cells are born.
  • Try a variety of starting configurations to see the variety of lives you can create.
